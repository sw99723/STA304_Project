# R-Based Multivariate Sampling Design & Selection Bias Calibration

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed and executed a rigorous statistical workflow to mitigate selection and non-response biases in large-scale observational and survey datasets. Designed advanced sampling architectures and executed multivariate modeling to ensure 100% data integrity and robust causal inference verified at a 95% confidence level. 

---

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities & Technical Contributions

### 1. Sampling Architecture & Simulation
*   **Methodology:** Stratified Random Sampling & Cluster Sampling
*   **Implementation:** Engineered custom sampling algorithms in **R** to address the limitations of Simple Random Sampling (SRS), ensuring the accurate representation of demographic sub-populations.

### 2. Bias Mitigation & Data Calibration
*   **Methodology:** Post-Stratification Weighting
*   **Implementation:** Formulated and applied post-stratification weights to calibrate raw datasets, effectively neutralizing non-response biases and selection errors to guarantee data 무결성 (data integrity).

### 3. Confounding Control & Multivariate Modeling
*   **Methodology:** Logistic Regression
*   **Implementation:** Implemented logistic regression models using R's analytical ecosystem to statistically control for confounding variables, executing precise hypothesis testing to uncover true variable relationships.
